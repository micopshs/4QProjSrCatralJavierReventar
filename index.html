VERBATIM

A Customizable Wordle Implementation

How to Play Verbatim

Guess the word within the specified attempts

Mechanics of Verbatim

  • Click the letters of your guess
  • Click enter to validate
  • Letters surrounded by this color mean that you found the correct letter in the correct place
  • Letters surrounded by this color mean that you found the correct letter in the wrong place
  • Guess again and again until you find the word
  • A lower guess count will increase your ranking in the leaderboard

The following features can be customized in the settings:

  • Word length, which ranges from 4-7 letters
  • Number of attempts, which ranges from [word length, word length*2] or can be infinite
  • Pool of words to pick from
  • Activation or deactivation of "correct letters in wrong place" feature
  • Color to indicate correct letters in right or wrong places
